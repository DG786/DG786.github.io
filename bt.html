Introduction

Hola Amigos! I hope you all are ready to embark your journey at this prestigious institute. Before you get into this voyage, it’d be better if you get familiar with your own discipline.

The Department of Biosciences and bioengineering, abbreviated as BSBE. Situated at core-3, this department, has its own identity. The place filled with bio-reactors? Autoclaves? And most importantly, RESEARCH? Stop right there. This is your department. Have a glance, because you’d be spending here, your next four years (technically 3 years, you know first year is common for all) of your graduation. Embrace it.

Biosciences and Bioengineering, as long as the name is appealing to you, so are the divisions which formulate it. Let’s give you a preview of four years of your life.

The Dark Past

TFirst of all, congratulations to all of you, for your JEE results and a hearty welcome to IIT Guwahati. I know what you’re thinking, “The Dark Past”? We all know we expected more, something better. Whatever it may be. The JEE result, or the 12th board results. And in some way, we weren’t able to achieve something we always aspired for. But, for one moment, just chuck those thoughts from your mind, and think, what if this could be the best thing happen to you.
In short, just move on. This is IITG.

Curriculum

The curriculum comprises of various courses, which many of you possibly haven’t heard of. You will get acquainted to your first core biology course in you second semester, Modern Biology-BT101. Then, the series of biotechnology courses awaits you, with a crisp of chemical engineering courses. These courses are designed in such a way, that you get a complete biotechnology experience in a more prodigious manner.

Since, almost all of us have no biology background in our high school, it is certainly difficult to develop interest in these courses. But eventually, things will become simpler and steady. And for those people, who are not able to develop overtime, they have the liberty to follow their own passion, in parallel to the academic courses. The lectures and labs are designed in such a way that a normal person can get ample amount of time, to learn and explore new things he/she always wanted to do.

So, trust me. You’ve made a right decision.

Branch Change

Many students come to IITs mainly with the aim of branch change.

Disclaimer: It takes a great deal of effort. While many of you, on reading this might feel a gush of confidence running through your veins, only four to five of you can realize the dream.Well at this point, I think it’s time to dig in the sculpture of our branch. Get familiar and espouse the things you already have, and at the same time, focus on the goals you want to achieve. Almost for all the people, this goal is BRANCH CHANGE.

Internship Opportunities

Most people in the department opt for internships at the end of the second year. There are two kinds of internships: Industrial and Academic. In case of Academic interns, the candidate is supposed to apply for various programs, facilitated by various research bodies in India including TIFR, IISc, NCBS-TIFR, CMBL, CBRI, CDRI etc., and abroad too. The students also have an option to take a project, under one of the professors in the institute. As far as the industrial interns are concerned, the students can apply in various companies, on the basis of their interests.

One should consider the fact that internships are awarded on the basis of a strong resume and skillset. In third year, many students apply for internships in bigger firms. Some people, to gain more research exposure, apply abroad too.

Career Options

Since, this branch is a research based field and considering the level of knowledge the students get in just four years, it becomes mandatory for the students to pursue higher studies, if they want to get into the core field. There are ample opportunities for R&D abroad and professors lend in their hands and recommendations play a huge role. As for the non-core is concerned, it is totally up to individual whether he/she wants to do CAT, IAS, Analytics, Photography and what not. The tag matters and so does the zeal for the passion. A lot of companies come for placements and offer great job opportunities to the students.

Summary

The department of biosciences and bioengineering might be something you never aspired for, but it is definitely something which will give you a different direction to you and your life.
